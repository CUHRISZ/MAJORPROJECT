• Whisk together egg yolks, cream, and vanilla in a small bowl until thoroughly combined; set aside.
  • Process flour, sugar, and salt in a food processor until combined, about 3 seconds. Add butter and pulse until a coarse meal forms, about 12 pulses.
    Add egg mixture and pulse until mixture starts to clump together, 10 to 12 pulses. Dough will be crumbly but should hold together when squeezed.
  • Transfer dough to a clean work surface, and press into a single mass. Divide dough evenly into 2 portions. Pat each half into a 1-inch-thick disk.
    Tightly wrap each dough disk in plastic wrap. Chill dough until firm, at least 2 hours or up to 24 hours.
